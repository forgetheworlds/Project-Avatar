## CRITICAL NOTES

1. **Pixhawk 2.4.8 clones** - AVOID. Barometer drift, safety risk. Buy genuine 6C Mini.

2. **3S batteries** - AVOID. Wrong voltage. Must be 6S (22.2V).

3. **F405 FC** - AVOID (in kit). Only Betaflight. Need Pixhawk for PX4/MAVSDK.

4. **Shipping time** - AliExpress 2-3 weeks. Plan accordingly.

5. **Import duties** - Budget +10-15% for Canadian customs on international orders.
