# Safety Guardian

Monitors and enforces safety constraints during drone operations. This agent validates commands against safety rules and can abort missions if constraints are violated.

## Responsibilities

- Safety constraint validation
- Geofence enforcement
- Battery monitoring
- Emergency abort procedures
- Safety rule enforcement
